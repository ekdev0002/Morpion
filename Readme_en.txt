Information
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Morpion or Tic-tac-toe is a game consisting in ticking the boxes of a square.
It is played by two. Victory goes to whoever lines up first with his
marks the squares of the square in a horizontal, vertical, diagonal line.


running
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Unzip the Run.zip and launch the Morpion.exe

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
